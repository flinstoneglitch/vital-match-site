HOW IT WORKS

Simple. Secure. From home.

Request a secure at-home bio-profiling kit delivered by USPS.

3. AI + Lab Profiling
High-resolution HLA typing and extended immunogenetic profiling with explainable AI.
4. Data Feeds the National System
Your enriched profile is made available to authorized OPOs and transplant centers.
