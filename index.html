How Vital Match Works

From your home to better data in the national system — with full consent.

1. Order Your Kit

Request a secure at-home bio-profiling kit delivered by USPS. Simple saliva or dried blood spot collection.

2. Send Your Sample

Collect at home and drop it in any mailbox. Our CLIA-certified lab partners handle processing with full chain-of-custody.

3. AI + Lab Profiling

High-resolution HLA typing and extended immunogenetic profiling. Our explainable AI builds rich compatibility profiles.

4. Data Feeds the National System

Your consented, enriched profile is made available to authorized parts of the existing national transplant system (UNOS, OPOs, transplant centers).
